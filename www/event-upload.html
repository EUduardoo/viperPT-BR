Upload a Match schedule

Instructions:

  1. Go to The Blue Alliance website (www.thebluealliance.com) and select the event.
  2. Right-click on the match schedule webpage and select 'View Page Source'.
  3. On the 'page source' view, select all of the content (Ctrl-a) and copy it (Ctrl-c)
  4. Connect to the scouting app and come to this page.
  5. Paste the content (Ctrl-v) in the text area below and click on 'Upload Event'
